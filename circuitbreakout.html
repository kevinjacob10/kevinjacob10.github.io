Project Overview

Circuit Breakout: Dual Polarity is a 3D puzzle-adventure game centered around real-time dual-character control and electrical logic-based gameplay. Players switch seamlessly between Data and Surge, combining mechanical interaction and electrical manipulation to navigate a derelict facility and solve environmental puzzles. The core challenge lies in designing responsive character switching, ability-driven puzzle mechanics and a clear UI that communicates complex electrical states and system feedback to the player.
